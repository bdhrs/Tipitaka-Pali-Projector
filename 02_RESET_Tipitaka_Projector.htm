• On old-aged devices or not a standard web browser: if you enable all dictionaries and/or bilingual language function at once, it may cause the device's browser crashing and you shall not be able to open the Pāḷi Tipiṭaka Projector.

  • It happens maybe because of your device/browser doesn't have enough memory or powerful enough to load many Mb of dictionary data at once. Try to reduce the dictionaries to load next time.

  • Use the above button to refresh Pāḷi Tipiṭaka Projector's Setting to its original state. And try openning Pāḷi Tipiṭaka Projector again.

  • If after doing so, it still doesn't work, you may wish to clear your browser's cache, browser's storage, and restart your device.

  • This app is mainly tested with Firefox Browser. Thus, it is advisable using this app with Firefox browser.
    • May you be well and happy!
